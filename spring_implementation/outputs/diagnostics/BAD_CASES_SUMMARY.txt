CASE 3: Sample3_3_11 (ref=16.95, pipeline=10.57, -38% error — BRIGHT CORES UNDERSAMPLED)
────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────

Root Cause: TOP-75% TRIM IS NOT AGGRESSIVE ENOUGH FOR THIS CELL
  - Cellpose mask has too much "dim filler" for this high-density cell
  - Pipeline mean (470.3) is too low for high-condensate cells
  - But top-25% (1314.9) nearly matches the reference (1361.65)!
  
Diagnostic Observations:
  - FOV: 45 × 131 × 127 (normal size)
  - Central nucleus: label 1 (11.8 pixels from center) ✓ looks correct
  - Mask has 57,116 voxels of cond & nuc
  - Intensity distribution shows bimodal:
    - Bright cores: 1314.9 (top 25%)
    - Dim halos: 470.3 (mean)
  - Reference (1361.65) clearly used tight manual boundaries
  
Fix Options:
  1. Use adaptive trimming based on intensity histogram shape (e.g., use top-25% or top-10% 
     when distribution shows strong bimodality)
  2. Switch from fixed top-75% to dynamic per-cell selection
  3. Use peak/mode instead of mean for cells with bright cores
